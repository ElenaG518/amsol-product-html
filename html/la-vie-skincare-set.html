Description

The La Vie en C Ultra-Active Skincare Set gives you two formulas rich in vitamin C -- to rejuvenate your skin naturally. La Vie en C Facial Cleanser removes dirt and excess oil from the surface of your face, without stripping away healthy moisture. La Vie en C Moisturizer Serum provides all-day skin hydration without leaving your face greasy or shiny. Ideal for both men and women. Perfect for all skin types, even acne-prone skin, adult rosacea, eczema or psoriasis.

Uses

Cleanser: With its mega-dose of vitamin C, this age-defying facial cleanser promotes healthier, brighter, younger-looking skin. By lifting away oil and dirt, it diminishes the appearance of blackheads and patchy breakout on the chin, along the hairline, and across the nose. Packed with powerful herbal ingredients, it helps reduce the red and inflamed look of acne-prone teenage skin and adult rosacea. Infused with calming lavender and rosehip fruit extract, it soothes and moisturizes the skin after shaving to prevent rash and razor burn.

Serum: This revolutionary stabilized vitamin C (as ascorbic acid) formula helps prevent signs of facial aging and keeps young skin looking firm and supple for as long as possible. Decreases the look of sagging skin, fine lines, coarse wrinkling, dark under-eye circles, and age spots (hyperpigmentation). Also a soothing moisturizer for acne-prone skin and rosacea. Calms the redness of a ruddy complexion. Even combats the damaging effects of sun exposure and outdoor free radicals.
